Mandate 01 / 05

A mandate in two halves — build, and hold.

Build

Original platforms.

We engineer original platforms for institutions whose workflows demand purpose-built infrastructure. Engagements are direct, scoped, and confidential. We do not publicise client work.

Hold

Indefinite stewardship.

We acquire established software businesses operating in regulated and mission-critical industries. Our hold period is indefinite. We do not flip, recapitalise for exit, or break up what we own. We are not a fund.
